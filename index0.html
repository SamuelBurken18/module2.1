Taco Pasta Salad

Ingrediants Directions
Pasta Boil pasta til soft.
Black Beans Drain pasta then add 1 cup of mayonnaise and 1/2 cup of sour cream.
Mayonnaise Mix in sour cream and mayonnaise. The add the Hidden Valley dip mix.
Sour Cream Mix all together until creamy and even.
Shredded Cheese Add cheese and black beans to the salad and stir them in.
Small Tomatoes Cut tomatoes into smaller pieces and add them in the salad.
Onion Dice one onion and add into the salad.
Hidden Valley fiesta ranch dip mix Let sit in fridge for 2 hours, and then enjoy.

Potato Salad

Ingrediants Directions
Potatos(10) Skin potatos and cut them up into small bites sizes.

Mustard Add a half cup of mustard to the mayonnaise.Stir until mixed
